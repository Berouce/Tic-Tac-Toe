Welcome to Tic-Tac-Toe!

Here's a quick summary of the instructions:

  • The game is played on a 3x3 grid.
  • Two players take turns to place their respective symbols (usually X and O) on the grid.
  • The objective is to get three of your symbols in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) before your opponent.
  • The game ends in a draw if the entire grid is filled without a winner.
  • To start, enter the names of both players and click "Start".

Best of luck and have fun!
